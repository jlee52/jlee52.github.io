Jeonghwan 'Jay' Lee

I earned my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin in 2022, under the mentorship of Dr. James Sulzer.

My doctoral research focused on

  • Design optimization of an affordable gait trainer
  • Biomechanical pre-cursor to predict spastic response with wearable exoskeletal assistance
  • Biomechanical phenotypes of post-stroke Stiff-Knee gait

Since completing my Ph.D., I am working as a Senior Robotic Engineer at Contoro Robotics, where I lead the development of robot perception software for the container unloading automation solution [video].
